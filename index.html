Air Series 700 Tent

Product Details

The Torpedo7 Air Series 700 inflatable tent is the instant solution to family camping



The biggest option in the Torpedo7 Air Series, this huge family tent with two detachable privacy pods can comfortably accommodate up to 8 people. Use the pods to partition off two rooms and a lounge area or leave them off for one large open plan living space. The Air Series 700 has a generous sized entrance area and plenty of windows and ventilation to keep the fresh air flowing.
 
Inflating this tent is quick and easy. No need to assemble any poles or put any pieces together, simply peg it out flat and inflate. Done. Four external valves are pumped up to 7psi each with the included pump complete with purge valve attachment so you have no fear of overinflation.
 
Features:
  • Compact footprint with plenty of interior space
  • Separate lounge area
  • Front and rear ventilation
  • Tall – 220cm head height in the bedroom pod
  • Easy setup – no separate parts or poles to assemble
  • Seam sealed fly and polyethylene floor
  • 3,000mm waterproof rating
  • Internal electrical light cable routing
  • 5x Air pillars – 7psi each
  • 1x Doors – large front entry
  • 1x Clear PVC side window
  • 2x Porch poles
  • Pump
 
Specifications:
  • Outer fly: 490cm x 770cm x 230cm (H)
  • Inner pod: 210cm x 280cm x 188cm (H)
  • Packed weight: 43kg
  • Packed size: 90cm x 54cm x 54cm
  • Frame: air bladder
  • Waterproof rating: 3000mm
  • Porch poles: 180cm x 16mm
 
Construction:
  • Outer fly: 150D Oxford Polyester
  • Inner pod: 63D breathable polyester
  • Floor: 120g/m2 Polyethylene
  • Porch poles: Steel
 
Please Note: The warranty for Torpedo7 tents does not cover wind damage. This is standard practice for all tents sold within New Zealand and Australia.
